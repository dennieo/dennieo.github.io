My products

Products I’ve designed, built & shipped.

Three products I took from idea to launch — two on the App Store and one full web platform — owning the concept, the experience and the design system end-to-end. Below them, a track record of work where I owned the design.

Numi

Food clarity, redesigned.
AI nutrition app · iOS In beta · TestFlight

My own product — an AI-powered nutrition tracker for iPhone that kills manual food logging. Snap a photo and it instantly analyzes the meal — calories, macros and micronutrients — while building a personal “Metabolic Fingerprint” for context-aware guidance. I designed and built it end-to-end: concept and positioning, the photo-logging flow, fasting, weight and hydration tracking, the insights engine, and a clean design system tying it all together.

Tysha

White noise for the 3 a.m. shift.
Baby-sleep app · iOS Live on the App Store

A white-noise app for new parents and those late-night wake-ups. It generates real-time white, pink and brown noise you can mix and save — no loops, no accounts, no internet, no ads. I designed and built it end-to-end around one-handed, instant playback: a layered sound mixer, saveable “Rooms”, a gentle fading sleep timer, Siri control and a Lock Screen timer — all fully offline.

Karta

Scan, eat, pay, go.
QR ordering & payments · Web platform Live · new in 2026

My newest product — one QR code for a whole restaurant. Guests scan the sticker on their table to browse the menu, order, split and pay in their own language, with no app to install. Behind it sits a full back office: real-time floor plan, menu editor, analytics, auto-routed payment splits between kitchen, bar and tips, and an AI “help me choose” waiter. I designed and built the whole system end-to-end — the guest web app, the owner dashboard and the design system tying them together.

About

Where design, AI & technology meet.

I’m a product designer with 14+ years designing and shipping digital products across web, mobile and emerging tech. My work sits at the intersection of product strategy, user experience and technology — turning complex problems into simple, intuitive experiences while balancing user needs, business goals and technical constraints.

At DraftKings, I design large-scale consumer experiences in a highly regulated, fast-moving environment — where usability, performance and consistency directly shape engagement and business outcomes.

Beyond the day-to-day, I actively build and experiment with AI-powered products — exploring how modern language models, intelligent systems and rapid-prototyping workflows can accelerate development and unlock entirely new experiences. My own products — Numi (AI nutrition), Tysha (baby-sleep sounds) and Karta (QR ordering & payments for restaurants) — came out of exactly that: concept to prototype to shipped. I’m drawn to work where product design, AI and innovation come together to solve meaningful problems — and make products that feel both useful and delightful.
